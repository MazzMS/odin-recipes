Milanesas

Description

The chocolate frappuccino is a delight. So delicious that it looks like a dessert. It has coffee, milk, chocolate and cream - perfect!

Ingredients

  • 1 double coffee
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 tablespoon of chocolate chips and extra for decoration
  • Ice
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate sauce (purchased or homemade ganache)
  • Whipped cream, for decoration
  1. Blend the coffee with ice
  2. Add the chocolate chips and the sauce together with the milk. Blend.
  3. Serve in tall glass and top with cream, chocolate sauce and grated chocolate.
