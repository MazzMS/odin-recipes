Milanesas

Description

This is a typical food in Argentina. There, the people eat it mainly in launch and dinner, although some of the Argentines eat it during the breakfast

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • Chopped parsley
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Bread crumbs
  • 2 pounds of buttocks
  1. Peel the garlic and chop together with the parsley.
  2. Place the eggs in a bowl and beat until well dissolved. Then add the parsley, garlic and season with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the meat in the previous mixture, leave for a few minutes, remove and dredge in breadcrumbs.
  4. In a frying pan with plenty of hot oil fry the milanesas. Remove and drain on absorbent paper.
